History

Rafting Adventures was founded in 2005 by a group of passionate river guides who believed that nature’s wildest waters should be accessible to everyone. What began as a small outfit offering weekend tours on local rivers has grown into one of the region’s most trusted whitewater rafting companies. Over the years, we’ve guided thousands of thrill-seekers through breathtaking rapids, forging memories that last a lifetime. Our commitment to safety, sustainability, and exceptional customer service has made us a favorite among families, adventure enthusiasts, and corporate groups alike.

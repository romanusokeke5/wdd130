Featured Trips

The Gentle Giant (Family Float)

Perfect for families and first-timers! This half-day trip offers calm waters, stunning scenery, and gentle rapids. Ideal for all ages to enjoy the beauty of the river without the intense thrills.

  • Duration: Half-Day (3-4 hours)
  • Difficulty: Class I-II (Mild)
  • Highlights: Wildlife viewing, swimming breaks, scenic views.

Wild River Rush (Intermediate)

Ready for more excitement? Our full-day Wild River Rush trip takes you through exhilarating Class III rapids. Paddle through exciting waves and experience the true thrill of whitewater rafting!

  • Duration: Full-Day (6-7 hours)
  • Difficulty: Class III (Intermediate)
  • Highlights: Challenging rapids, riverside lunch, teamwork.

The Canyon Conqueror (Advanced)

For experienced adventurers seeking the ultimate challenge! This multi-day expedition tackles Class IV-V rapids in remote canyon sections. Requires good physical condition and prior rafting experience.

  • Duration: 2-Day Expedition
  • Difficulty: Class IV-V (Advanced)
  • Highlights: Big drops, remote wilderness, overnight camping.

Sunset Serenity Float

A relaxing evening float trip, perfect for unwinding after a long day. Experience the river's tranquility as the sun sets, casting beautiful colors over the water and surrounding landscape. Includes light refreshments.

  • Duration: Evening (2-3 hours)
  • Difficulty: Class I (Very Mild)
  • Highlights: Sunset views, peaceful atmosphere, refreshments.
